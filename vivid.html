Portfolio Project

Vivid E-commerce Website

The website serves a diverse range of products and membership plans, encouraging users to explore and purchase with ease.





Design Goal

The primary goal of this e-commerce platform is to create a vibrant, intuitive shopping experience, offering users a seamless journey from product discovery to checkout.


Typography

Montserrat typography, chosen for its modern, clean design, provides readability and style across all UI components.



Color

With a mix of golden yellow (#FFD700) as the primary color and black accents, the design conveys luxury and modernity, enhancing engagement and confidence in the platform.



Vivid Filter Functionality

The filter functionality enhances the shopping experience, allowing users to refine search results by categories, price range, brand, color, and more. The filters are easy to toggle on or off, with golden markers indicating selected criteria. This feature is essential for users to quickly find specific products, making their shopping experience efficient and personalized.



Vivid Details Page

Each product details page offers an immersive experience, blending high-resolution images, descriptions, and specifications with a clean, modern layout. The golden-yellow highlights draw attention to key elements like pricing, available discounts, and “Add to Cart” buttons. Users can read reviews, view related products, and access a detailed overview, ensuring they have all the information needed to make confident purchasing decisions.



Vivid Checkout Page

The checkout page is designed to be streamlined and user-friendly, ensuring customers can complete purchases quickly and securely. A clear breakdown of items, pricing, and delivery options is paired with an intuitive payment gateway. Golden accents highlight key actions like “Proceed to Payment” or “Place Order,” providing a luxurious feel and guiding users effortlessly through the final steps of their purchase.



Vivid Order Tracking

The order tracking page offers transparency and ease, enabling users to view the status of their orders in real-time. A visually engaging timeline, accented in the brand’s golden color, displays stages such as “Processing,” “Shipped,” and “Delivered,” giving users peace of mind and a clear understanding of their order’s journey.



Components

The website incorporates a variety of reusable UI components, such as buttons, input fields, modals, and tooltips. Designed in Montserrat font, with bold golden accents, these elements provide consistency across pages, reinforcing the brand identity and ensuring smooth user interaction.
