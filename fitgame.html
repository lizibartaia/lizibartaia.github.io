Portfolio Project

Fitgame Fitness App

FitGame is a fitness app designed to motivate users to reach their health and fitness goals by combining the benefits of gamification with personal fitness. Recognizing that sustaining a workout routine can be challenging, FitGame turns exercise into an engaging game, rewarding users for their consistency, progress, and achievements.





Project Description

FitGame is a comprehensive fitness app that empowers users to reach their health goals through a fully customized workout experience. The app offers video-based training sessions tailored to individual fitness levels and goals, allowing users to follow along with expert-led exercises. In addition to workouts, FitGame provides valuable information on nutrition and diet, helping users make informed choices to fuel their progress. With added challenges and a gamified structure, FitGame keeps users motivated and engaged, creating a fun and effective journey towards a healthier lifestyle.


User Personas

The user persona for this delivery app centers around convenience-focused individuals who prioritize quick, reliable service and seamless app navigation. These users often juggle busy schedules, valuing features like real-time tracking, personalized recommendations, and flexible payment options. They expect an intuitive, efficient UX/UI design that allows them to order, track, and receive deliveries with minimal effort, making the app a go-to tool for managing daily tasks on the go.



John is a 45-year-old CEO who leads a fast-paced lifestyle. With long work hours and frequent travel, he finds it challenging to stay consistent with his health and fitness goals. However, maintaining his physical well-being is essential for John as it helps him stay energized and focused in his demanding role.

That's where FitGame comes in. FitGame provides John with personalized workout plans and video-based training sessions he can do anytime, anywhere. The app also allows him to track his health metrics, set individual fitness goals, and receive tailored insights on nutrition to complement his routine. With FitGame, John can seamlessly integrate fitness into his busy schedule, keeping him on track to achieve his health goals and manage his well-being more effectively.
