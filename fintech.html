Get Verified with eKYC

To provide you with a secure and seamless banking experience, Finllet uses eKYC (electronic Know Your Customer) for quick and reliable identity verification. With eKYC, you can verify your identity online in just a few minutes, without the need for lengthy paperwork or in-person visits. This simple process helps protect your account and ensures compliance with financial regulations, so you can bank confidently.



Just follow the steps to upload your ID, take a quick selfie, and provide a few personal details. Our secure system will verify your information swiftly, getting you one step closer to full access to Finllet’s features. Let’s get started on securing your account!
