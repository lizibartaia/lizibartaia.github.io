Portfolio Project

Noshify Delivery App

Created a seamless platform where users can order both food from restaurants and groceries from markets, providing convenience and variety in one app. With personalized recommendations, scheduled delivery options, and real-time order tracking, Noshify provides the ultimate convenience for satisfying cravings and stocking up on essentials.





Description and Problem

Noshify provides the ultimate convenience for satisfying cravings and stocking up on essentials. The problem Noshify solves is the inconvenience and inefficiency of using multiple apps for food and grocery delivery.
Prior to Noshify, users had to switch between various platforms to order restaurant meals and grocery items, leading to a disjointed and time-consuming experience. With Noshify, customers can access both restaurant and market delivery services in one centralized app.


Design Process

Identify competitors, and analyze user preferences and pain points. Define the objectives of the project. Generate ideas for features, functionalities, Create a visually appealing and cohesive design language, Conduct usability tests with real users.





User Personas

The user persona for this delivery app centers around convenience-focused individuals who prioritize quick, reliable service and seamless app navigation. These users often juggle busy schedules, valuing features like real-time tracking, personalized recommendations, and flexible payment options. They expect an intuitive, efficient UX/UI design that allows them to order, track, and receive deliveries with minimal effort, making the app a go-to tool for managing daily tasks on the go.



User Journey Map

The user journey for the delivery app begins with discovery, where users find the app through various channels like ads or social media. Once intrigued, they download and register, completing onboarding by setting up delivery addresses and payment preferences. With everything in place, users explore the app, browse products, and add items to their cart, selecting preferred delivery options and confirming payment to place an order. Afterward, they track the order in real-time, watching as it progresses toward their location. Finally, the journey concludes with delivery confirmation, where users receive their items, rate the experience, and may even receive follow-up recommendations or offers, reinforcing app loyalty.



Authentication User Flow

The authentication user flow begins when a user opens the app and is presented with the login screen. If they are a returning user, they can enter their credentials, such as email and password, and click the "Login" button. If the credentials are valid, they are granted access to the app's features. For new users, there is an option to sign up, directing them to a registration screen. Here, they provide necessary information, including their name, email, and password. After submitting the registration form, a confirmation email may be sent for verification. Upon confirming their email, the new user can log in using their credentials.





Color,Typography and Icongraphy

The app features Inter as its primary typography, offering a clean and modern look. Complemented by a warm pink background, this color scheme creates an inviting and friendly atmosphere, enhancing readability and user engagement throughout the interface.





Design

The app includes a user-friendly interface where users can effortlessly choose from various categories, such as cuisine types or meal options. The main screen showcases visually appealing food images, allowing users to browse and select their desired items easily. An order tracking screen provides real-time updates on the status of their delivery, displaying estimated arrival times and delivery progress. Additional screens feature personalized recommendations, special offers, and a history of previous orders, ensuring a comprehensive and engaging user experience throughout the app.





Download the App

This app is fully optimized for both mobile devices and tablets, ensuring a seamless experience across screen sizes. With a responsive UX/UI design, users can enjoy smooth navigation and consistent functionality, whether on a phone or tablet. The app is available for download on both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store, providing easy access and compatibility for Android and iOS users alike.
