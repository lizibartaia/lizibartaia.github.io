Portfolio Project

CRM

Our CRM (Customer Relationship Management) platform is designed to streamline business interactions, enhance productivity, and foster better client relationships. The system features a comprehensive suite of tools focused on essential areas like communication logs, order management, contract management, and invoicing.





Design Goals

The UI/UX design will emphasize intuitive navigation, organized layouts, and responsive elements to ensure users can effortlessly navigate between categories, track client activities, and access documents. Our goal is to create a user-centered design that supports efficient data management and fosters better client relationship insights.

Color

Our CRM platform’s color scheme is thoughtfully crafted to balance functionality with a modern, engaging aesthetic. With a primary color of #1100ab and a secondary color of #6a56b8, the design establishes a professional and cohesive identity, emphasizing key interactive elements and promoting visual hierarchy.
The background and main components are set in light warm tones to provide a welcoming, unobtrusive environment that makes content easy to navigate and read.

Neutral Colors: #a0a3bd and #d9dbe9: These soft, neutral tones are integrated throughout for borders, dividers, and less prominent elements, offering balance and focus on primary content. Black and White for Text: Black and white provide excellent contrast, ensuring readability and clarity across all text, with white often serving as the main background for text-heavy sections. This palette combines warmth, clarity, and professionalism to enhance user experience, maintaining focus on essential content while giving users a visually appealing interface to interact with.
